About Me

I’m a full stack web developer and e-commerce specialist interested in web interface and user experience. I have experience in HTML, CSS, Vanilla JS, AJAX, and Java. I graduated with a BS in Computer Science Information Systems from Kean University, New Jersey in 2014. I have been working in the web-development and e-commerce management field for about 4 years.

My passion is turning any complex situations (I enjoy a good challenge that leads me to one-on-one talks with my computer) into elegant interface designs that pleases the eye of the viewer.

Whenever I’m not expanding my knowledge on programing languages and having code-talks with my computer, I like going on long walks with my loving dog Luna and taking pictures, specially of her and her furry friends, lots and lots of them.
